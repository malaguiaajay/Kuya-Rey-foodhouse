About

Kuya Rey Food House and Catering Services is a restaurant and catering business in Poblacion, Kidapawan City, Philippines.

We offers good services, nice decor and affordable prices.

We have a Facebook page where we posts our menu, photos, and updates. Some of the dishes we serves are chicken curry, pork sisig, beef caldereta, pancit canton, and lechon kawali.
